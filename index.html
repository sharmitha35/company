Wipro Introduction

Wipro Limited is a market leading Information Technology, consulting and business process services company. Wipro utilizes the power of analytics, robotics, cloud and other emerging technologies to help clients spread across six different continents to adapt to the current digital trend and help them thrive to success. Wipro has over 1,60,000 dedicated employees serving together and building a bold and new future. The amount of revenue generated by Wipro in the financial year 2017 is 848 crores USD.
